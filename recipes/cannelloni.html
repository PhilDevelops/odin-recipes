Spinach & Ricotta Cannelloni

Delicious cannelloni

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tin Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Spinach
  • Ricotta
  • Cannelloni Tubes

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a small pot over medium high heat. Add garlic, onion. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes until the onion is translucent. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
  2. Add tomatoes, stock, sugar, salt and pepper. Stir then simmer on medium low for 20 minutes.
  3. Mix Filling (Ricotta and Spinach) in a large bowl
  4. Transfer Filling to a piping bag with a large nozzle (that fits in the tubes), or use a strong ziplock bag. Or do this step using a knife (it's a bit tedious though!). Pipe the filling into the tubes.
  5. Place cannelloni in baking dish. Pour over remaining Sauce, covering all the tubes.
  6. Cover with foil, then bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Remove foil, scatter over parmesan then mozzarella. Return to oven for 15 minutes until cheese is melted.
