Ham & Cheese Toasted Sandwich

Delicious & Cheesy!

An absolute classic that anybody can basically whip up on those cold mornings!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Spinach Leaves
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Using a bread knife, cut 8 thick slices from the bread. Place 4 slices of bread on a chopping board. Spread each with butter.
  2. Top with ham, cheese and spinach. Season with salt and pepper. Top with remaining bread slices. Butter outside of each sandwich
  3. Heat a large heavy-based frying pan over medium heat. Toast sandwiches for 2-3 minutes each side until golden brown and cheese is melted
  4. Cut toasties in half and serve with mayonnaise on the side.
